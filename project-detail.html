S3 Oxygen Generation Plant at Bangalore

We are happy to announce our 50LPM oxygen generator based on Indian Institute of Science ( IISc ) technology . With focus on helping hospitals have a cost effective solution for installing oxygen generation plants to combat COVID - 19 and to develop step down care facilities which can play an important role in a health care system . The 50LPM can help in high flow oxygen therapy ( HFOT ) which is a form of respiratory support used in the hospital where oxygen , often in conjunction with compressed air and humidification , is delivered to patient at rates of flow higher than that delivered in oxygen therapy and also reduces the work of breathing .
